# Blueprints

## Customization

You may customize the blueprints to add additional information about your products. Make sure to add a **new section** for the additional fields and **DO NOT** change the handle or the order of existing fields. ****If you do, things might blow up.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Do not change the **handle** or the **order** of existing fields.
{% endhint %}

## Assets Container

The asset container for the product images defaults to `assets`. You may change this to any container you want.

# Blueprints

## Additional Fields

You may customize the blueprints to add additional information about your products. Please make sure to add a **NEW SECTION** for the additional fields and **DO NOT** change the handle or the order of existing fields. ****If you do, things will blow up.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Add additional fields to a **new section.** Do not change the **handle** or the **order** of existing fields.
{% endhint %}

## Assets Container

The asset container for the product images defaults to `assets`. You may change this to any container you want.
